Align data in cells

You can align your data horizontally and vertically within a cell. To do that,

  1. place the cursor to the position where you want the alignment to be applied (this can be a cell or already entered data),
  2. select the horizontal alignment type you would like to apply clicking the Horizontal Alignment icon situated at the top toolbar:
    • use the Align Left option to align your data by the left side of the cell (the right side remains unaligned);
    • use the Align Center option to align your data by the center of the cell (the right and the left sides remains unaligned);
    • use the Align Right option to align your data by the right side of the cell (the left side remains unaligned);
  3. select the vertical alignment type you would like to apply clicking the Vertical Alignment icon situated at the top toolbar:
    • use the Align Top option to align your data to the top of the cell;
    • use the Align Middle option to align your data to the middle of the cell;
    • use the Align Bottom option to align your data to the bottom of the cell.

If you wish your data in a cell to fit the column width select a cell and click the Wrap Text icon situated at the top toolbar. If you change the column width, data wrapping adjusts automatically.
